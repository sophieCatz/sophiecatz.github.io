Warrior Cats

Warrior Cats is a book series about feral cats that live in clans. The main clans are ThunderClan, RiverClan, WindClan, ShadowClan, and SkyClan. There are also groups of cats that do not belong to any clan, such as loners, rogues, and kittypets. The series is written by Erin Hunter, which is a pseudonym for a group of authors. The series has over 80 books and has been translated into many languages. The series has also been adapted into a manga series and a graphic novel series.
